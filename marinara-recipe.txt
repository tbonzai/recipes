Marinara sauce - 8 servings (enough for 1 lbs pasta)
2 - 28 oz cans petite diced tomatoes
1 - 12 oz can tomato paste
1 - medium onion diced very fine (1/4") or chopped in food processor
4 - cloves fresh garlic sliced very thin or diced very fine
2 - tbsp. olive oil
2 - tbsp. thyme
1 - tbsp. basil
1 - tbsp. Italian seasoning
2 - tbsp. sugar
12 oz water
Salt

Heat olive oil in pot. Add onion, garlic, thyme, basil and Italian seasoning. Saut√© over medium high heat until onions are translucent. Crush tomatoes using a potato masher retaining all the juice including that which was in the can. Stir in water, sugar, tomato paste and diced tomatoes with their juice. Bring to boil then reduce heat to low and simmer for one hour. Salt to taste.
